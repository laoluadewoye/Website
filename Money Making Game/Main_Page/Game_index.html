Welcome to the Money Game!

Rules

  • You start with a 1000 dollars in your hand.
  • You can do a variety of actions. One action lets a day pass.
  • To do an action, press the corresponding button. A menu for the button will appear.
  • You can look at multiple menus at once, but once one of the menus is used it resets and updates to the next day.
  • Every seven days you get a weekly check of either 100 dollars or a custom amount of your choosing.
  • The aim is to gain as much money in 365 days. Go big as much possible before the time is up or you stop the program!
  • Only use integers to submit values for simplicity.
  • If you ever need to access this page again, there will be a button in the top left corner in the game. However, DO NOT RESET THE GAME PAGE OR YOU WILL LOSE YOUR PROGRESS!

Are you ready? Push the button if you are!
